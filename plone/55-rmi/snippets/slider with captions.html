Short Courses, Education, Outreach


We offer short courses on everything from wine tasting, mead making, pairings of bugs and beer, olive oil sensory evaluation, honey, as well as lectures from industry leaders, authors, James Beard Award Winners, and world-renowned UC Davis Faculty.

A World Class Institute for Wine and Food Sciences


The Robert Mondavi Institute for Wine and Food Science is home to the foremost departments of Viticulture and Enology, and Food Science and Technology in the world, and houses the only (?) LEED Platinum winery, brewery, and food processing facilities in the world.

A Vibrant Community of Student Involvement and Support


Through connections with industry leaders, The Robert Mondavi Institute procures research grants and fellowships for graduate students in the fields of Food Science and Technology, and Viticulture and Enology.
